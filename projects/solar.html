Solar Automation System

Optimizing solar field operations through real-time data tracking and workflow automation.

Industry

Clean Energy

Role

Automation Architect

Tech Stack

IoT Core, Next.js, API

The Challenge

Managing large-scale solar installations involves complex maintenance schedules and manual data tracking. The client wanted a system to provide high operational visibility and automate process optimization tasks.

Fragmented Maintenance
Relying on manual logs for panel cleaning and repairs.
Low Visibility
Difficulty in identifying low-performing strings in real-time.
Operational Overheads
High costs associated with manual site reports.

Operational Map Preview

The Solution

I developed an end-to-end automation platform that handles maintenance scheduling and real-time performance tracking.

Workflow Automation
Triggered maintenance tasks based on performance drops.
Real-time Dashboards
Instant visibility into energy production trends.
Smart Reporting
Automated daily and monthly operational summaries.
