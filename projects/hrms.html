HRMS & Payroll Automation

A comprehensive SaaS platform designed to centralize employee data and automate complex payroll cycles.

Industry

HR & FinTech

Role

Full Stack Developer

Tech Stack

Next.js, Supabase, SQL

The Challenge

Traditional HR management often relies on fragmented spreadsheets and manual entry, leading to high error rates in payroll and attendance tracking. The client needed a unified system that could handle large employee bases while ensuring 100% accuracy in financial disbursements.

Fragmented Records
Employee data scattered across multiple legacy files.
Payroll Errors
Manual calculation of bonuses, deductions, and taxes.
Attendance Gaps
Inconsistent attendance tracking due to reliance on manual or semi-digital inputs.

Core Architecture

The Solution

I developed a custom HRMS engine that integrates attendance, payroll, and employee lifecycle management into a single dashboard.

Automated Payroll
One-click salary generation considering all variables.
Attendance Engine
Centralized attendance tracking with manual and system-based input support.
Document Vault
Secure storage for employee contracts and KYC.
Self-Service Portal
Employees can access salary slips, submit attendance requests, and manage leave records.
