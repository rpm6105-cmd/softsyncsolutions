HRMS & Payroll Automation

A custom internal platform designed for teams handling attendance, payroll, leave workflows, and employee records without relying on scattered spreadsheets or repetitive manual processing.

Client Type

Operations-heavy business

Use Case

HR, attendance, payroll

Scope

Custom internal tool

Stack

Next.js, Supabase, SQL

The Challenge

Payroll and HR operations were slowed down by disconnected records, manual calculations, and inconsistent attendance input. The team needed one system that could reduce processing effort, improve visibility, and support employee operations without introducing more admin overhead.

Fragmented Data
Employee records, attendance inputs, and payroll logic were split across multiple tools and files.
Manual Month-End Work
Payroll preparation required repetitive cross-checking, adjustments, and high-effort review cycles.
Poor Operational Visibility
Managers lacked one reliable place to review attendance, leave status, and employee payroll data.

What We Built

We designed a centralized HRMS workflow that brought employee records, attendance capture, payroll logic, and self-service actions into one operational system.

Attendance Engine
Centralized attendance tracking with support for operational exceptions and cleaner input handling.
Payroll Automation
Salary generation flows designed to reduce manual work around deductions, payouts, and review steps.
Employee Self-Service
A simple interface for salary slips, leave workflows, and employee record access.

Delivered Scope

The build focused on practical internal operations rather than feature sprawl.

  • Central employee master and role-based access
  • Attendance workflow and review management
  • Payroll processing flow with cleaner operational handoff
  • Leave tracking and employee-facing actions
  • Operational dashboard for admins and managers
