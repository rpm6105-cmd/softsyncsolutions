Compliance Management System

A workflow-driven system built to track statutory deadlines, documents, filing status, and follow-up actions for teams managing high-volume compliance operations.

Client Type

Compliance-focused operation

Use Case

PF, ESI, KYC tracking

Scope

Workflow and document system

Stack

Next.js, Supabase, APIs

The Challenge

The team was handling statutory deadlines, client documents, and filing status through manual follow-up and fragmented records. That created unnecessary operational risk, poor visibility, and too much time spent chasing updates rather than managing outcomes.

Deadline Risk
Missing documents and unclear status created risk around PF and ESI filing timelines.
Manual Follow-Up Load
Teams were repeatedly chasing the same document gaps through email and ad hoc communication.
No Single View
There was no reliable dashboard showing what was pending, submitted, filed, or overdue.

What We Built

We built a status-driven compliance workflow platform that gave the team a central source of truth for documents, deadlines, and filing progress.

Status-Based Tracking
Clear workflow stages for pending, submitted, filed, and follow-up scenarios.
Automated Alerts
Reminder logic to surface missing documents and upcoming deadlines earlier.
Operational Dashboard
A centralized view across clients, tasks, and current compliance status.

Delivered Scope

The system was designed to reduce risk, improve accountability, and make high-volume follow-up more manageable.

  • Client and document tracking workflow
  • Deadline visibility and reminder triggers
  • Centralized status monitoring across entities
  • Reporting layer for pending and filed work
  • Workflow structure for repeatable compliance execution

Business Impact

200+

Clients supported in one workflow

0

Missed PF or ESI deadlines post-launch

100+

Manual follow-up hours saved each month
